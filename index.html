The Three F's

What we stand on

Every player, every parent, every program — built on the same three pillars.

F

Faith

We coach with conviction. Character shapes performance — on the field, in the classroom, in life. Discipline, humility, and the long view.

F

Family

We partner with parents, not replace them. Every athlete is someone's son or daughter first. Communication is constant; the standards are shared.

F

Furtherment

Move them forward — next level of play, next classroom, next business idea. We're in the development business. You leave better than you arrived.

Meet the Founder

Brian E. Kennedy II, M.Ed

Providence '17 M.Ed #12 Friars 2× Big East Champion

Born and raised in Upland, California, Brian played Division I soccer at Providence College, earning back-to-back BIG EAST Championships before graduating in 2017 and returning to complete his Master of Education. He founded The Ballers Kingdom in 2019 to merge everything he learned on the field, in the classroom, and in the Inland Empire community: a training environment that develops athletes into leaders and entrepreneurs.

"One degree. Two rings. Not done yet." — Brian's graduation cap, Providence College
