About the Founder

Brian E. Kennedy II, M.Ed

Division I soccer champion. Educator. Coach. Founder. Built The Ballers Kingdom to develop the next generation on and off the field.

Providence '17 M.Ed #12 Friars 2× Big East Champion Upland, CA

Brian Edward Kennedy II was born and raised in Upland, California. He played Division I soccer at Providence College in Rhode Island, wearing #12 for the Friars in the BIG EAST Conference. In his four years at Providence, Brian helped bring home back-to-back BIG EAST Championship rings, graduating in 2017 with a Bachelor of Arts and returning to complete his Master of Education.

After Providence, he came back to Southern California with a clear mission: take what he learned at the Division I level — the discipline, the training systems, the leadership — and make it available to the youth of the Inland Empire. The Ballers Kingdom was founded in 2019 as the vehicle for that work.

"One degree. Two rings. Not done yet." — From Brian's graduation cap, Providence College

The philosophy

Brian's coaching is built on three pillars — Faith, Family, and Furtherment. Faith gives character; family gives foundation; furtherment moves every player forward. That framework runs through every private session, every youth clinic, and every Youth Venture Program cohort he leads.

Beyond the field

Brian also serves as Entrepreneur Ecosystem Director at AmPac, where he supports minority and underserved business owners across California, Arizona, and Nevada in accessing capital and building sustainable ventures. The Ballers Kingdom is a piece of that larger mission: develop leaders young, and keep developing them for life.

By the numbers

M.Ed and B.A., Providence College · 2× BIG EAST Champion · Founded The Ballers Kingdom 2019 · Based in the Inland Empire, serving California youth athletes and their families.
