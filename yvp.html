Youth Venture Program · YVP

Next-gen entrepreneurs start here.

A summer and after-school program teaching ages 10–17 the language of money, business, and leadership — through hands-on venture building.

Why YVP exists

Most young people never get taught the language of business until they're already in the workforce — and by then, decades have been lost. At The Ballers Kingdom we believe financial literacy, entrepreneurial thinking, and leadership should be taught as early as reading and math.

YVP is the program we built to make that real. We combine curriculum, mentorship, and real venture experience so that by the time a student finishes the program, they've launched something of their own.

Who it's for

Ages 10–17 — middle school and high school students curious about business, money, leadership, or starting something of their own. No prior experience required.

Curriculum Pillars

Four areas. One entrepreneur.

Every YVP cohort moves through the same four foundations — the skills we wish we'd learned sooner.

$

Financial Literacy

How money works. Income, expenses, saving, investing, credit, taxes. The actual fundamentals — not the hype.

B

Business Fundamentals

Value, pricing, customers, revenue, cost, margin, simple accounting. How a business actually runs.

L

Leadership & Communication

Public speaking, professional writing, negotiation, presentation. How to lead a team — and yourself.

V

Venture Build

Students design, build, and launch a real small venture by the end of the program. Pitch day with real feedback.
